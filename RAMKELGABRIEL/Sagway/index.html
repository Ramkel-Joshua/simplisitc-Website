Segway and Kenya

Documenting my trip around the US on my very own Segway.

June 02, 2017

My first day of the trip! I can’t believe I finally got everything packed and ready to go. Because I am on a Segway, I wasn’t able to bring lot with me. I was only able to bring the essentials such as Cell Phone, iPad, digital Camera, and a protein bar. However, as Lee Tan would have said, journey of a thousand miles starts with one step Segway.

July 14, 2017

I saw some interesting things on the side of the road today. “Passing cars when you can’t see, may get you a little glimpse of eternity.”

I definitely won’t be passing any car.

August 20, 2017

Well I made it 1200 miles already and I passed through some interesting places on the way: Limuru, Nakuru, Naivasha, Machaskos, and many other places.
